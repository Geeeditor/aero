Base Maintenance

Our base maintenance capabilities in Europe and Asia cover the full spectrum of services, including complex projects with integrated solutions from FL Technics subsidiaries and partners.

Our maintenance capacity and capabilities vary depending on the region.

Explore our certificates and approvals in relation to each company and area.

  • Component Repair & Overhaul
  • Non-destructive testing methods (D1 rating)

Specializing in narrow-body commercial aircraft services, FL Technics provides EASA and FAA-approved maintenance solutions.

  • Scheduled base maintenance checks for commercial and regional jets.
  • Defect rectification incl. major metal & composite structural repairs.
  • End-of-lease checks, parking & storage, modifications.
  • Aircraft refurbishment and interior conversion.
  • Full aircraft paint support.
  • Engineering support.
  • Tool rental services.

Our workshops have the capability to perform component maintenance, repair, and overhaul for the following groups of components:

  • Air conditioning & pressurization.
  • Doors & hatches.
  • Electrical power.
  • Equipment.
  • Engines – APUs.
  • Flying controls.
  • Pneumatics.
  • Protection; ice, rain, and fire.
  • Eddy current testing.
  • Ultrasonic testing.
  • Magnetic particle testing.
  • Penetration testing.
  • Thermography testing (Water ingress method).
  • Engine and APU borescope inspection (B1 rating)

Our LEAN-based approach to base maintenance.

Following LEAN-based principles, your aircraft is maintained through our BAY Production System. This is a unified, comprehensive set of rules, guidelines, tools, and techniques for more efficient turn-around times.

We apply our expertise and best practices around the world, including base maintenance hangars in Europe, expanding infrastructure in the Asia Pacific, and facilities under development in the Americas.

Cabin Services

Passengers often judge an aircraft by the appearance of the cabin. In fact, their initial impressions can directly impact your business.

Your aircraft’s state-of-the-art avionics may go unseen but problems such as broken seats and damaged sidewalls are far more likely to be noticed by passengers onboard. Our cabin services team ensures that a cabin’s quality, style, and condition reflect the quality of the rest of the aircraft, and your brand.

These services are available alongside our international line maintenance offering, making us your ideal ‘one-stop-shop’ MRO. Our cabin services are available globally, while our experienced technicians are approved and certified under our EASA Part-145 approval. They are certified to provide a range of services including seat repairs, cabin modifications, galley removals/refits, and upgrades to in-flight entertainment and surveillance systems. Our team can assess your requirements and tailor maintenance, upgrades, and modifications to meet your business needs.

Get in touch with our team to discuss your cabin service requirements.

Engine Repair Shop

Our engine repair teams are based in Europe with a purpose-built quick turnaround activity shop in Lithuania. FL Technics provides engine repair and maintenance solutions for CFM56 type engines (CFM56-3, CFM56-5B, CFM56-7B).

Our maintenance capacity and capabilities vary depending on the region.

Explore our certificates and approvals in relation to each company and area.

Explore

  • Engine BSI / External examination
  • Engine Repairs
  • Engine Preservation
  • Lease Return & Storage

Thorough borescope & external engine inspections. Our experienced team carries out engine examinations using state-of-the-art equipment and provides clients with comprehensive reports.

Reliable maintenance services for the most popular engine types in the market, with a focus on quality, safety, and short turnaround times. Our service offering includes both engine maintenance & repair.

Whether you require an on-demand service or part of a lease return process, our dedicated facility specializes in aircraft engine preservation. We guarantee engine preservation for both long and short term periods at our in-house engine shop in Kaunas, Lithuania. We meticulously store preserved engines in a controlled environment with regulated temperature and humidity.

Full range of services, including financing, administration, and storage of aircraft engines. We offer flexible solutions that fit our client's requirements.

Modern facilities & an owned quick turnaround activities shop.

FL Technics Engine Services provides EASA, FAA, and UK CAA certified solutions including engine maintenance, repair, and storage. Our teams are located in Europe with a dedicated shop facility in Lithuania, in close proximity to Kaunas International Airport (KUN).

Line Maintenance

The FL Technics team boasts extensive capacity and capabilities which leverage the worlds’ largest independent line maintenance network with 100+ stations across Europe, EMEA, Asia Pacific, and North America.

Our maintenance capacity and capabilities vary depending on the region.

Explore our certificates and approvals in relation to each company and area.

  • Transits & Line A checks
  • AOG Support Services
  • On-Call Services
  • Line Station Setup

Our experienced engineers carry out thousands of routine checks per week. This depth of experience allows us to handle unexpected occurrences in an efficient and safety conscious manner, ensuring that your aircraft spend more time doing what they do best; adding to your bottom line.

FL Technics works side-by-side with airlines to carry out comprehensive evaluations and deliver precise, safe, and efficient AOG support. If your business is suffering an AOG, our team work as quickly as possible to assess, diagnose, and deliver a solution.

Our on-call maintenance support can be set up in any location an airline operates from, with efficient and effective implementation, deployment of engineers, and tooling.

Due to our international approvals and wealth of experience, we can establish new line stations at short notice. Our expertise, engineers, tooling, and equipment are on constant standby to be supplied to any station worldwide.

The world’s largest independent line maintenance network.

FL Technics teams provide round-the-clock comprehensive line maintenance services with AOG services available 24/7. Our international approvals include EASA Part 145, Federal Aviation Administration (FAA), UAE (United Arab Emirates) General Civil Aviation Authority, QCAA Qatar Civil Aviation Authority and Bermuda Department of Civil Aviation among many others, spanning numerous aircraft types.

Through FL Technics' own network and partners, we can supply a comprehensive range of aircraft components.

We can perform engine replacements for our customers at our own line stations or at a preferred location anywhere in the world.

FL Technics teams deliver engineering services in support of paint inputs and throughout all phases of aircraft repainting.

Working alongside our airline and MRO partners, we perform component recovery, cleaning, and inspections. We also perform EASA Form 1 re-certification on all major and minor components including engines, APUs, LRUs and more.

AOG services available 24/7.

We can quickly assemble an AOG support team of skilled engineers and dispatch them anywhere in the world to support serious AOG or provide aircraft recovery services. Additionally, our teams are equipped with a mobile line station (vehicle with tools) which can be dispatched to any location within the EU at short notice.

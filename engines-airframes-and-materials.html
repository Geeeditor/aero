Engines, Airframes, & Materials

Professional time critical solutions for component and material support for airlines, MROs (Maintenance, Repair, and Overhaul), and other industry players. Being an ISO 9001:2015 certified organization, we offer spare parts consumables, rotables supply, various engine support and trading programs, and high value asset management solutions. Our services also include quick engine turnaround capabilities from the FL Technics Engine Services team.

A wide range of aircraft components for sale, exchange, and lease.

With warehouses in London, Frankfurt, Jakarta, Singapore, Miami, Vilnius, and Kaunas, we provide spare parts and component supply as well as repair & management services for almost any type of aircraft in your fleet.

FL Technics provides cost-saving programs for airlines, including engine material and component repair management, which includes landing gear overhaul.

We supply a wide range of aircraft components for sale, exchange, and lease. Our stock is constantly expanding due to numerous aircraft teardown projects. In addition, we provide one-stop-shop component repair solutions covering inspection, testing, repair, overhaul, and modification for all types of aircraft spare parts. All services are provided using our in-house capabilities, as well as in partnership with a vast OEM network and independent EASA/FAA certified repair workshops.

We stock an extensive range of parts and materials across our warehouse sites. We also partner with more than a thousand manufacturers and suppliers to maintain our reliably supplied warehouse network.
